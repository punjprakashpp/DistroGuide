About US

Linux distribution come in all shapes and sizes. In addition to the top distros that are designed to appeal to a wide number of users, there are a whole lot of specialized distribution that are built for a particular purpose.

What is Linux Distribution?


A Linux distribution (often abbreviated as distro) is an operating system made from a software collection that is based upon the Linux kernel and, often, a package management system. Linux users usually obtain their operating system by downloading one of the Linux distributions, which are available for a wide variety of systems ranging from embedded devices and personal computers.
